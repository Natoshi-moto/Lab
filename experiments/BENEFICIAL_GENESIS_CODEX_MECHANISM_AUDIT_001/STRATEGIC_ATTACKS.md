# Strategic attacks

- Whale + charity rebate: private profitability threshold is `token_multiple + rebate*access > opportunity_cost + friction`; retention falls with paid rebates. Magnitude is empirical.
- Exchange + timing: omnibus custody aggregates allocation and any coupled votes; privileged denominator/cutoff information changes irreversible entry utility.
- Tainted funds + rebate: low private opportunity cost plus access expands profitable regions while recovery/compliance harms are external.
- Sybil + governance: concave/capped genesis allocation is defeated before identities can receive, delegate, transfer, or aggregate votes.
- Undersubscription + speculation: a minimum-unit sole donor takes the pool. Coordinated price response is `UNABLE_TO_VERIFY`.
- Miner + denominator shock: inclusion/censorship around cutoff changes relative shares. Real probability/magnitude are `UNABLE_TO_VERIFY`.
- Charity-set capture + side agreement: exact-script checks cannot establish retention, independence, or use.
- Secondary accumulation + token-weighted governance: genesis caps do not survive transfers; exchanges, coalitions, and delegators compound concentration.

Actor coverage includes small donors, whales, Sybils, custodians, miners, honest/colluding/donor-controlled charities, tainted-source controllers, speculators, accumulators, coalitions, and delegators. Cryptographic detectability is limited to on-chain predicate violations; side agreements are at best statistically/socially auditable and often out of protocol.
