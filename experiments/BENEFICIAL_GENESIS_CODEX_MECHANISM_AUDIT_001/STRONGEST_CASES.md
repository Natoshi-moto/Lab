# Strongest evidence-bound cases

## Continue

The bounded receipt and fixed-pool arithmetic are deterministic, supply-conserving, and reproduced by 37 design tests plus the evidence gate. Charity payment can be cryptographically coupled to a destination claim, nullifiers prevent duplicate use in the synthetic model, and exact linear allocation is actor-split invariant before floors. None of the economic countermodels proves that every possible integration is harmful: non-transferable recognition, no governance, minimum participation gates, and explicit exclusion/attestation policies can preserve a bounded research path.

## Stop or radically redesign

The current product has not specified any function that needs transferability. A sole minimum contributor receives the entire pool; linear allocation imports whale concentration; alternatives that redistribute per identity are Sybil-exploitable; token-weighted governance and secondary accumulation convert economic concentration into control; and the verifier intentionally accepts cryptographic control without proving lawful ownership or charity retention. Welfare reverses sign across unresolved parameters. A simpler receipt or no-token design performs the specified donation/recognition functions with fewer structural risks.

The second case controls any launch or economic-gate decision. The first supports only a bounded redesign experiment, not the current transferable direction or real-world readiness.
