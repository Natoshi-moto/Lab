## A. Strongest evidence-bound case for **continuing** Beneficial Genesis

### Core claim

The **charitable migration receipt** concept — prove source control, pay exact genesis charity scripts, bind a PQ destination, allocate a finite pool by verifiable weights — is **internally coherent** as a research mechanism and is **not shown self-defeating** under honest, non-collusive participation with non-transferable or carefully gated claims.

### Supporting evidence (typed)

1. **Mathematical supply safety** of floor pro-rata + unissued remainder (design + independent probes). Class: math / reproduced.  
2. **Fail-closed admission residual map** already separates crypto-proven facts from charity honesty, rebates, and legal title (design threat model). Class: repository observation.  
3. **Program objective** (issue #33) is multi-track research with economic red-team as a gate, not a launch authorization. Class: policy/program framing.  
4. **Linear pro-rata is Sybil-stable** for allocation quantity (independent + prior econ agreement). Class: math.  
5. **Rebate and tainted “collapse” are not unconditional theorems** — severity depends on access friction and opportunity cost (independent grids; breaker conditions). Class: conditional model.  
6. **Continuation with conditions** matches differential post-freeze reading of econ breaker/retest disposition without copying their prose into this seat’s freeze. Class: post-freeze differential agreement on mechanism disposition.

### What this case does **not** claim

- Transferable token is necessary.  
- Economic gate pass for mainnet.  
- Rebates will be rare.  
- Whales are fine under token-weighted governance.  
- Legal clarity.

### Conditions that keep this case alive

- Specify ledger functions before defending float.  
- Prefer non-transferable / delayed transfer if F5/F7 not required.  
- Do not sell concave/cap as permissionless fairness.  
- Keep gov decoupled from raw allocation or use durable identity.  
- Treat rebate/tainted as social-legal residuals with explicit nonclaims.  
- RESEARCH_ONLY until real Bitcoin + real PQ + counsel tracks progress.

## B. Strongest evidence-bound case for **stopping or radically redesigning**

### Core claim

As soon as the product is read as a **transferable fixed-pool allocation** that **rewards donation** under **permissionless identity-free** rules, the design faces a **hard trilemma**: it cannot simultaneously deliver (i) no identity layer, (ii) anti-whale concentration via formula, and (iii) low secondary-market / tainted-resale risk. Combined with out-of-protocol rebates and fixed \(P\) independent of \(D\), the socially marketed story (“charity + fair migration”) is **under-determined at best and adversary-shaped at worst**.

### Supporting evidence (typed)

1. **Countermodel: concave and caps without identity are Sybil-broken** — large measured gains in independent probes (`03`, `04`, `11`). Class: math / reproduced. **This countermodel controls** any “just use sqrt/caps” repair.  
2. **Transferability not necessary for F1–F4** yet is the main amplifier of tainted resale and post-genesis re-concentration. Class: mechanism structure.  
3. **Whale concentration is definitional under pro-rata** — 99% sats → 99% pool (`01`). Class: math. Not an “attack”; a property.  
4. **Rebate can zero charity net** under full access; secret whale access already extracts large share (`07`, `08`). Class: conditional; worst-case structural.  
5. **Fixed pool / floating ratio** makes undersubscription a speculative prize and oversubscription dust — unstable participation game (`06`). Class: structural.  
6. **Simpler alternatives dominate on F1 alone** (direct donation) and on F1–F4 without float (non-transferable receipt). Class: comparison under held functions.  
7. **Crypto cannot see the worst social attacks** (rebate, title, side deals) — residual list is load-bearing, not cosmetic. Class: design residual.

### What would falsify this stop/redesign case

- A demonstrated necessary function of transferability **within declared v1 scope**.  
- A permissionless anti-concentration mechanism that survives Sybil **without** identity (no known candidate here).  
- Credible exogenous enforcement that bounds \(\alpha\) near 0 and blocks tainted monetization **without** abandoning F4.  
- Empirical evidence that participation is overwhelmingly non-mercenary and charity nets are robust (not currently available in-repo).

### Redesign directions this case recommends

1. **Non-transferable or delayed-transfer** default for v1 claims.  
2. **Accept pro-rata whale concentration** as proportional sacrifice **or** add an explicit identity/credentials layer (and drop pure permissionless marketing).  
3. **Decouple governance** from economic units.  
4. Revisit **fixed \(P\)** vs issuance tied to verified charity flow.  
5. External **AML/legal** track before any public solicitation narrative.

## Adjudication (this seat)

Both cases are **evidence-bound**. The stop/redesign case **controls** the transferable + anti-whale-without-identity subdesign. The continue case **controls** the underlying charity-bound migration receipt **if** product scope stays narrow and conditions bind.

**Aggregate mechanism verdict:** `CONTINUE_WITH_CONDITIONS` — not unconditional continue, not full economic gate pass, not crypto-level reject of the receipt idea.
