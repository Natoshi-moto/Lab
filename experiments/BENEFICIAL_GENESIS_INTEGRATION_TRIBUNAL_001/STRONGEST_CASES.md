# Strongest cases — BGEN-INTEGRATION-TRIBUNAL-001

Both cases below are steelmanned from repository evidence only.

## A. Strongest evidence-bound case to CONTINUE

1. **The finished layer is genuinely finished.** The allocation/claim
   arithmetic has been implemented at least three structurally different
   times (design pack Python, RETEST_002 Node, mechanism-audit
   reconstructions), differentially tested to 43/0/0 on the fixture
   corpus, and survived this tribunal's fresh random probing (T-011) and
   boundary probing (T-013/T-014). No executable counterexample to the
   normative core exists anywhere in the record after seven adversarial
   packages tried.
2. **Every alleged fatal economic flaw degraded under scrutiny into a
   conditional or a policy choice.** Whale concentration → disclosure +
   scheme choice; Sybil → identity stance; rebate/taint → parameter-region
   conditionals whose sign flips with unmeasured variables; governance →
   separable. Nothing in the record proves the mechanism self-defeating.
3. **The one defect that survived integration (TRIB-F-001) is in an
   analysis model's input validation** — not in the normative protocol,
   which fails closed on the analogous surfaces — and its repair is small,
   bounded, and fully specified (R1).
4. **The next steps are cheap, decisive, and falsifying.** All three
   mechanism-facing audits converged (from fresh sessions) on the same
   experiment: specify product functions and run the regime matrix. If no
   design serves the required functions within proven constraints, the
   record's own stop-condition fires. Continuing is the
   uncertainty-minimizing move even for a skeptic.
5. **The process demonstrably self-corrects.** Nine real defects caught
   and repaired in the historical rounds; a tenth (TRIB-F-001) caught by a
   fresh seat; each repair independently retested. The pipeline finds its
   own errors, which is the property that matters most at research stage.

## B. Strongest evidence-bound case to STOP or radically redesign

1. **The causal premise has zero evidence after this much work.** The
   entire point — a transferable reward inducing *additional* charitable
   giving — is unmodelled, unmeasured, and was invisible to every seat
   until an epistemic audit named it (TRIB-F-010). Years of adversarial
   simulation never touched the one variable that determines whether the
   mechanism helps or merely redistributes. A research program that
   cannot yet state its treatment effect's sign may be optimizing
   machinery for a null effect.
2. **Every proven property describes a failure surface that scales with
   success.** Rebate capture, taint laundering value, collusion
   profitability, and governance capture all grow with token value and
   volume (TRIB-F-016). The record proves the pathways exist and cannot
   bound their incidence. Success makes the proven risks bigger, not
   smaller.
3. **The transferable design is weakly dominated on the evidence in
   hand.** Both mechanism audits find every specified function achievable
   without transfer, and transfer is what powers each attack pathway. The
   honest redesign — non-transferable receipts — removes most of the
   "genesis token" concept; what remains is a donation-receipt registry,
   which may not need this apparatus at all (the no-token baseline in the
   record is never shown inferior on any required function).
4. **The only fully autonomous full-seat verdict in the pre-audit record
   was REJECT_OR_REDESIGN** (PR #40's counterevidence row), and the
   record's standing disposition was operator-prescribed before repair
   (TRIB-F-017). The convergence since is real but was produced inside
   one operator's framing, one account, and shared prompts (TRIB-F-018).
5. **Sole-donor capture and dust exclusion (TRIB-F-014)** show the fixed
   pool misallocates at both extremes; fixing it pushes toward
   flow-linked issuance, which is a different mechanism — i.e., the
   redesign case, arrived at from arithmetic alone.

## Adjudication of the disagreement space

- **Genuine disagreements:** none remaining at verdict level after
  TRIB-F-013 (the EVIDENCE_STATE split is question-indexed) and after
  TRIB-F-001 superseded PR #43's package pass. The A/B tension above is
  not a factual disagreement — it is a **different objective function**:
  A optimizes information gained per unit of bounded work; B optimizes
  not building capacity for net-harm mechanisms. Both are consistent with
  every confirmed finding.
- **Unresolved empirical premises:** participation elasticity, flow
  composition, rebate-access distribution, token value, liquidity
  (TRIB-F-010/016). These decide between A and B and cannot be settled
  synthetically.
- **Shared blind spots:** combined attacks; post-transfer governance
  dynamics; charity-sector behavior; the modeling frame itself
  (PROCESS_ADJUDICATION, shared premises).
- **Smallest discriminating experiments:** (1) the S1 product-function
  specification + regime matrix — if the no-token/non-transferable column
  satisfies every required function, B's redesign conclusion follows
  *within the record's own method*; if a required function genuinely
  needs transfer, A survives with a justified default reversal. (2) The
  S6 preregistered elasticity design — a near-zero or adverse induced-flow
  result fires the record's own stop condition.

Both discriminators are already the record's declared next steps, which is
itself the strongest sign the program is being run honestly.
