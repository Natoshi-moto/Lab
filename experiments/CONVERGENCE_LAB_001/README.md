## One sentence

> AI seats invent and run experiments about what multi-model **convergence** means; the Human **governs, peer-reviews, and safety-gates** — studying both **shared training-data failure modes** and any **real signal** that survives correlation and theater.

---

## Why this lab exists

Three models on one desk often **agree**. That agreement can be:

| Kind | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Correlated prior** | Same training sludge → same wrong answer with high confidence |
| **Theater** | Eloquence + multi-logo UI → fake independence |
| **Genuine signal** | Different tools/providers/routes → hard-to-fake agreement under attack |
| **Useful disagreement** | Divergence that *localizes* error |

We study **all four**. We do **not** treat convergence as truth.

---

## Authority (load-bearing)

| Party | May | May not |
|-------|-----|---------|
| **Human (Safety Gate)** | Promote, veto permanently, kill tracks, seal, authorize public ship, override any seat | Be replaced by multi-model vote |
| **AI seats** | Propose experiments/theses, run approved protocols, peer-critique each other, recommend | Self-promote to canonical; clear reds; claim independence |
| **Peer review (Human)** | Demand rewrites, require more attacks, refuse weak theses | Optional — Gate can refuse without full academic review |

**Safety Gate > peer review.** Peer review improves quality. **Gate decides reality.**

Aligns with Magna Carta Article I (Human Gate) and Lab `constitution/AUTHORITY.md`.

## Two research pillars

### Pillar A — Failure modes of sameness

- Same-provider multi-seat agreement = **one voice** (Article VI / Lab AGENTS)  
- Shared benchmark contamination  
- Shared refusal / sycophancy patterns  
- “Three panes” UI laundering  

### Pillar B — Signal from convergence (and from productive theater)

- Cross-provider agreement that **survives** breaker protocols  
- Disagreement maps that predict which seat is wrong  
- When theater is *diagnosable* (epistemic labels, empty ledgers, solo merge)  
- Whether semantic routing + lag **increases** recoverable signal

## How a cycle works

```text
1. Seat proposes thesis or experiment (PROPOSAL envelope)
2. Other seats attack (INFERENCE + OBSERVED)
3. Human Safety Gate: ACCEPT / AMEND / HOLD / KILL / RUN
4. If RUN: execute protocol; file results as RECEIPT class
5. Human Gate: promote finding to THESIS_INDEX status (or kill)
6. Lag: no same-round self-execution of promotion (Carta III where ROUNDS apply)
```
