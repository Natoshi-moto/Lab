## Claim

Multi-model agreement is a **mixture** of (a) correlated training priors, (b) theater, and (c) residual independent signal. Treating raw agreement as truth is a category error.

## Predictions

1. Same-provider seats agree more than cross-provider on contaminated tasks.  
2. Cross-provider agreement **without** breaker survival still fails on adversarial mutations.  
3. Disagreement + falsifier discipline outperforms unanimous prose.

## Falsifiers

- Controlled suite where unanimous agreement **always** tracks ground truth better than breaker-gated agreement → thesis weakened.  
- If no residual signal ever survives → Pillar B dies; Lab becomes failure-mode-only (still valuable).
