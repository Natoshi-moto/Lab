## Layers (from LOOM v0.1 spirit)

| Layer | Role |
|-------|------|
| 📜 RECORD | Verbatim immutable evidence |
| ⚖ CLAIM | Assertions that cite records only |
| 🧭 ROUTE | Course, handoffs, indexes — **no authority** |

Tooling (`loom verify` etc.) may still be unbuilt. **Process is mandatory now**; automation is roadmap.

LOOM v0.1 remains a parked proposal historically (PR #93 era) with open GITBRAID tension — this pack **elevates process intent**, does not close that engineering conflict.
