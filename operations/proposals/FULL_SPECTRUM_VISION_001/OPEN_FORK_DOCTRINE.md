# Open fork doctrine

**Status:** `PROPOSAL / STATUS_AUTHORITY: NONE`  
**Cites:** CLAIM-METH-005, OP-17  
**Genesis:** `baseline-001`

---

## Operator intent

**OP-17:** Defensibly fork and “steal” from open source; give back research when possible; build cool shit.

Street meaning: stand on giants without shame.  
Legal meaning: obey licenses, keep attribution, no proprietary laundering.

---

## Rules

1. Prefer clear OSI licenses (MIT/Apache/BSD-class where possible).  
2. Keep LICENSE/NOTICE; pin upstream commit in `VENDOR.md` when code is vendored.  
3. Rewrite product identity under Full Spectrum constitution.  
4. Give back: patches, skills, research notes, threat models — when low-cost and real.  
5. Hermes, Tails ideas, Qubes ideas, wallet open designs: **attribute lineage**.  

---

## Non-claims

- Fork rights ≠ trademark rights (cannot call a fork “Tails” as official).  
- Give-back is not a royalty obligation cosplay; it is culture.
