      FULL SPECTRUM UNIVERSITY

      The course is not beside Mithub. Using Mithub is the course.

      Every public experiment becomes a lesson; every reproduction becomes teaching material; every failure earns a place in the map. Learn by leaving inspectable wreckage.

      GATEWAY 00

      Permission to think messily

      Separate raw thought, interpretation, evidence and decision.

      Practice: write one claim you believe and one observation that could prove you wrong.

      MODULE 01

      Fork as a research instrument

      Preserve provenance, read the licence, state the divergence, run the comparison.

      Practice: document upstream, licence, hypothesis, changed variable and non-affiliation before modifying anything.

      MODULE 02

      Break claims, not people

      Design hostile tests that produce evidence rather than heat.

      Practice: steelman a claim, identify its load-bearing mechanism, then publish a reproducible failure condition.

      MODULE 03

      Meaning and mechanism

      Force the story, code, receipts and limitations to point at each other.

      Practice: attach one stable claim ID to the prose, implementation and verification receipt.

      MODULE 04

      Earn canonical attention

      Turn useful chaos into a small, boring, reviewable proposal.

      Practice: reduce the experiment to a clean diff, evidence bundle, risks and reversible adoption plan.

      • Raw drafts stay in your browser.

      🚨 Making it real is separate

      • A proposal is created, never silently adopted.
      • Checks, evidence and human review are required.
      • Canonical reds remain visible.
      • Snapshots remain immutable.
