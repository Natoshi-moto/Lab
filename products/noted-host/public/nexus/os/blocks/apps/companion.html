Welcome to Companion 👋

      
A canvas for thinking before you have words for it.


      
Companion is a place to dump half-formed thoughts, link them, and let the AI help you find what you're actually trying to say. Here's the short version:


      
1. Make notes

      
    
        
  • Double-click anywhere on the canvas to make a new note.
    • 
        
  • Type freely. Don't try to be tidy. Half-thoughts are the point.
    • 
        
  • Drag the edge of a note to draw a line connecting it to another.
    • 
        
  • Highlight text inside a note to color-code it. The legend button lets you label what each color means to you.
    • 
      


      
2. Send it to an AI

      
    
        
  • When the canvas has enough thought in it, click ⬆ brief.
    • 
        
  • Click copy brief for AI → — that puts a full Markdown summary on your clipboard.
    • 
        
  • Paste it into a chat with Claude, GPT, or whatever AI you use. Ask the AI to help you articulate, plan, critique, or synthesize.
    • 
      


      
3. Useful keyboard tricks

      
    
        
  • Double-tap Ctrl to lock menus open while you look at your canvas. Double-tap again to unlock. (A 🔒 chip appears top-right when locked.)
    • 
        
  • Esc closes any open menu (when not locked).
    • 
        
  • Hold Ctrl while moving the mouse to reveal extra controls in the corners.
    • 
      


      
4. About these modes

      
You're currently in noob mode: bigger buttons, hover tooltips, this guide on first open. When you feel comfortable, switch to calm mode — same app, smaller and more minimal. The chip in the bottom-right corner toggles between them.
