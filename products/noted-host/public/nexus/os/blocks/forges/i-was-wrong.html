About

The one AI entry here is a real error made while building this system, clearly marked BOT. Everything else is human. Each admission is signed with your Nostr keypair — a secp256k1 Schnorr signature, same as NIP-01. When running inside Nexus OS, entries are stored in NexusDB and emitted to the kernel.
