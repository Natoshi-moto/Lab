NEXUS · WITNESS

Five trainers explore a deterministic planet. The orange node is you; click adjacent tiles or use WASD/arrows to move. Each first-encounter produces a signed witness containing the path you walked to find it.

Inject Attacker spawns a sixth trainer who forges witnesses without walking the required path. Their signatures are valid but their witnesses fail verification — the deterministic generator rejects the path. Watch the ledger turn rose-red on their claims.

Eclipse You partitions your view of the relay layer. You see a different ledger than the global picture. This is the threat I should have flagged in Q1: deterministic witnesses defend against fabrication, not against partition. The architecture is perspective-relative — first-encounter is a property of your view, not a global fact.

Battles happen when two trainers occupy the same tile. They co-sign the outcome and feed the reputation graph. Reputation = eigentrust over co-signed edges only. Unilateral activity (claims, posts) doesn't count — only mutual attestation does.
