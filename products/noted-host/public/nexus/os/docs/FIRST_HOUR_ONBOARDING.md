# First Hour Onboarding

## Purpose

Sweep 16 turns the Sweep 15 Living Forge foundations into a readable first-session path. The target is not a forced tutorial. The target is a player who always understands what they are seeing, what can be changed, what accepting does, where their creation went, and what to try next.

## First-session route

1. Start in First Contact.
2. Reroll a live companion and live world.
3. Lock either side when it feels right.
4. Use Multiforge for nine live options when the player wants directions instead of sliders.
5. Accept contact.
6. The world becomes the animated desktop.
7. The companion appears bottom-left.
8. Home Notes can be sent into Companion.
9. Library receives the player-made specs.
10. Wallet explains that the player starts at 0 NEX.
11. Battleforge teaches attack motion as anticipation, strike, impact, recovery.

## First Contact canon

First Contact must answer these questions:

- What am I looking at? A live companion and a live home world.
- What can I change? Reroll, lock, open nine options, or fine tune.
- What happens if I accept? The companion and world are saved, the desktop changes, and the companion wakes on screen.
- What does not happen? No NEX is granted.

## Desktop arrival

The desktop is the selected world. The animated environment canvas is the primary atmosphere; CSS gradients are fallback. The live companion is a small inhabitant, not a decorative icon.

## Companion

The bottom-left companion should provide a one-time callout and a visible Home Notes handoff. It is local notes/memory for now. Do not claim AI assistant behavior until API assistant wiring exists.

## Wallet

Wallet onboarding copy must stay canonical:

- one wallet,
- one visible NEX balance,
- player begins at 0 NEX,
- proof outputs are not separate balances.

## Forge tools

Multiforge is the fast creation surface. Lattice Shell and Environment Forge are fine-tune surfaces. Compose Stage is Battleforge-style attack authoring.

## Non-goals

- No sound system.
- No AI assistant API.
- No new economy rewards.
- No wallet balance grants.
- No forced modal tutorial.
